Why MountCare Mumbai?

The Center of Excellence for Brain Cancer at MountCare Mumbai is dedicated to providing the most advanced brain cancer care. We offer the latest diagnostic techniques, including advanced MRI imaging, PET scans, and genetic testing, to ensure accurate and early detection of brain tumors.

Our specialists work together to determine the most appropriate treatment plan for each patient, including surgery, radiation therapy, chemotherapy, targeted therapies, and immunotherapy.

MountCare Mumbai is a leader in minimally invasive neurosurgery, using advanced techniques such as robotic surgery and endoscopic procedures to remove tumors with the least amount of disruption to healthy brain tissue. We also specialize in stereotactic radiosurgery, a non-invasive treatment that delivers focused radiation to tumors with precision.

In addition to medical treatment, we provide emotional support and rehabilitation services to help patients during their recovery. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures that all aspects of the patient's care are addressed, from diagnosis to treatment and post-treatment care.

The Center of Excellence for Brain Cancer at MountCare Mumbai is located in the heart of Mumbai, with state-of-the-art facilities for both diagnosis and treatment. We are committed to providing the highest standard of care and support for individuals affected by brain cancer.
