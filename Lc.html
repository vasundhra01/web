If you or a loved one is diagnosed with lung cancer, MountCare's Center of Excellence for Lung Cancer is committed to providing the highest level of care. Our multidisciplinary team specializes in both early detection and advanced treatment options, offering personalized care plans for each patient.



Why MountCare Mumbai?

MountCare Mumbai provides the latest advancements in lung cancer diagnosis and treatment. Our specialists offer comprehensive care using the latest imaging techniques, minimally invasive surgery, and cutting-edge therapies like immunotherapy and targeted therapy.

We collaborate with leading lung cancer research institutes to offer clinical trials and innovative treatment options for our patients.

Our expert surgeons and oncologists work together to create an individualized treatment plan that minimizes side effects and improves patient outcomes.
