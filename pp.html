If you or a loved one has prostate cancer, you want the very best care. MountCare's Center of Excellence for Prostate Cancer offers the latest approaches to diagnosis and treatment. Why MountCare Mumbai?

The Center of Excellence for Prostate Cancer at MountCare Mumbai provides the most up-to-date diagnosis, treatment, and management of prostate cancer. Our facilities are state-of-the-art, and as part of a full-service health system, we offer seamless referrals when needed. Our doctors and researchers are known for innovation in emerging treatments, with special expertise in open, laparoscopic, and robotic prostate surgery.

We provide comprehensive treatment approaches, including focal therapy that targets specific cancer cells. We collaborate with leading cancer institutes to offer advanced therapies through clinical trials.

MountCare Mumbai is at the forefront of radiation treatment, offering high-precision therapies such as brachytherapy and intensity-modulated radiation therapy (IMRT). These techniques effectively target cancer tissues while protecting surrounding healthy tissues.

Under the leadership of renowned urological surgeon Dr. Batra, our center has pioneered advanced robotic prostatectomy techniques that minimize nerve damage and enhance post-surgical recovery. We also specialize in surgical procedures that help patients regain urinary control and minimize discomfort post-surgery.

The Center of Excellence for Prostate Cancer at MountCare Mumbai offers services across multiple locations in Mumbai, including our dedicated cancer care facility in the heart of the city.
